Clayton State Administrative Offices

Clayton State administrative offices support the mission of the University. We assist in making student dreams real through the resources and services we provide the Laker community.

  • Academic Affairs

    The Division of Academic Affairs oversees Clayton State’s academic programs and policies. Dedicated to the support of our community, the office provides many of the faculty-development and student-success resources available on campus, such as the enrollment management, financial aid, admissions, advising and retention, academic success center and the library.

  • Student Affairs

    The Division of Student Affairs supports student learning and success through quality programs, services, and initiatives. We are committed to providing resources to all students, ensuring their learning experiences extend beyond the classroom.

  • Business and Operations

    The Division of Business and Operations provides essential fiscal, operational, and administrative support in order to ensure continued successes in teaching, research, and service. Our resources and services serve a variety of campus needs, such as public safety, accounting, dining services, and student accounts.

  • University Advancement

    The Division of University Advancement informs, involves, and invests. We tell our shared Clayton State story and involve alumni in our campus community. We find better ways to invest in programs that help students navigate through the college journey and into their careers.

  • Information Technology Services

    The Division of Information Technology Services provides hardware and software support to the campus community. We answer computer technology questions and house critical support services such as enterprise applications, the technology infrastructure department, and printing services.
