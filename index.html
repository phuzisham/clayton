If you desire to become tomorrow’s technology innovator, educator, and industry leader, then Clayton State University is the right choice. Our College of Information and Mathematical Sciences (CIMS) offers several pathways to realize your dream of becoming an IT professional in a digitally-driven world.

Find your areas of interest!

Associate Degrees

Begin to develop the framework for understanding how computer science and information technology works or build the necessary skills to enter an engineering school.

Learn More about the Associate Degrees

Bachelor’s Degrees

Earn a well-rounded degree that can prepare you for careers in IT, computer science, mathematics, or teacher education.

Learn More about the Bachelor’s Degrees

Undergraduate Minors

Enhance your degree with specific courses in mathematics, data, computer science, and web development.

Learn More about the Undergraduate Minors

Pathways to Engineering

Take the first steps toward becoming a highly-knowledgeable professional in the engineering field through one of several degree options through a partnership with Clayton State and our consortium of partner schools.

Learn More about the Pathways to Engineering

Master’s Degrees

Become a highly-qualified professional in digital archiving and electronic management or gain the knowledge–and certification–to teach mathematical competency at the secondary grade level.

Learn More about the Master’s Degrees

New Academic Initiative: Collaboration with the Georgia FinTech Academy

Did you know that more than 70% of all credit card transactions are processed through Georgia? Clayton State University has partnered with the Georgia FinTech Academy to allow students to take transfer elective credits in financial technologies (FinTech), instead of other elective options, that will prepare them to meet the workforce needs of this high demand area. (Pending approval by SACSCOC)

Learn More about the FinTech Academy
